julianYears numJulianYears

Full Usage: julianYears numJulianYears

Parameters:
    numJulianYears : float

Returns: Duration

Construct a Duration from a given number of [Julian years][julian_year]. A Julian year is defined as exactly 365.25 days, the average length of a year in the historical Julian calendar. This is 10 minutes and 48 seconds longer than a Gregorian year (365.2425 days), which is the average length of a year in the modern Gregorian calendar, but the Julian year is a bit easier to remember and reason about and has the virtue of being the 'year' value used in the definition of a light year. Duration.julianYears 1 --> Duration.days 365.25 [julian_year]: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Julian_year_(astronomy) "Julian year"
